# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Shipping investors, asset managers, and vessel buyers/sellers evaluating Range Shipping as a counterparty for S&P brokerage, fleet acquisition, and large-volume dry bulk fixtures. They arrive with a specific deal or mandate in mind. They are scanning for institutional credibility, deal experience, and commercial depth. The decision they leave with is whether to make contact.

## Product Purpose

Range Shipping's marketing landing page. Its job is to signal institutional authority in the dry bulk sector — establishing Range Shipping as a serious, experienced operator worth contacting for vessel transactions and chartering. Success is an inbound inquiry from a qualified counterparty.

## Brand Personality

Institutional, global, modern. The feel of a tier-1 financial institution that happens to operate ships at scale: clear hierarchy, authoritative typography, confident restraint. References: JP Morgan, Maersk, Cargill — not for their aesthetics but for their register: a site where serious people transact serious business.

## Anti-references

- Legacy maritime broker sites: clipart, port directories, Dreamweaver-era layouts, blue-sky stock photography.
- SaaS/startup visual language: gradient blobs, glassmorphism-as-decoration, product-hunt card grids, rounded corners everywhere, anything that reads as a software company or growth-stage brand.
- Anything generic enough that it could belong to any industry.

## Design Principles

1. **Deal credibility before aesthetics.** Every design choice earns its place by signalling the kind of organisation that completes transactions worth tens of millions. Beauty is a byproduct of clarity and weight, not decoration.
2. **Show scale, not features.** The product is the operator's track record and fleet — not a UI. Numbers, routes, vessel classes, and counterparty trust matter. Features and bullet lists do not.
3. **Restraint over spectacle.** The cinematic scroll animation is the one expressive moment; everything after it should land with authority, not with effects. Motion earns trust when it stops.
4. **Precision in every detail.** Investors read detail. Loose copy, misaligned grids, or inconsistent spacing signals operational sloppiness. Nothing is approximate.
5. **Global without being generic.** The world-spanning reach is real and specific — port names, tonnage figures, trade routes — not illustrated with abstract globe icons or stock photography of anonymous harbours.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. The animation-heavy hero must respect `prefers-reduced-motion` by cross-fading or skipping to the final frame instead of running the sequence. Body text contrast ≥ 4.5:1 against all backgrounds. The dark-on-dark palette requires particular vigilance on muted label text.
